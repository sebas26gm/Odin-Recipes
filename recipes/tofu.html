Sweet and sour Tofu Recipe

Description

Delicious vegan recipe. A block of tofu is seasoned and marinated, and then fried to serve with whatever sides you'd like!

Ingredients

  • Fresh chives
  • Sesame seeds
  • Maple Syrup
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Grounded ginger
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Chop the tofu into small chunks.
  2. Add into a bowl the syrup, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger and a bit of oil. Mix fully until uniform mixture.
  3. Add the sauce into a bowl with the tofu. Let the tofu marinate at least for 3 hours.
  4. Preheat a pan (a wok pan could work best) with sesame oil. Add the tofu, let it fry until golden.
  5. Once tofu is golden, add the chives and sesame seeds. .
  6. Your tofu is now ready! Serve with whatever side you'd like. Or even better, make a poke bowl with it! Enjoy :)
