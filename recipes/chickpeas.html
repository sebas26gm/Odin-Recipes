Chickpea Recipe

Description

This recipe is for a natural Chickpea recipe similar to a soup. The recipe includes several vegetables to add flavour, and you add chickpeas for a declicious seasoned soup!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (2 small tomatoes)
  • One Red Pepper
  • One Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • One carrot
  • One potato
  • Powdered Pepper
  • Salt
  • Natural tomato souce
  • Hot sauce (Optional)
  • Jalapeno (Optional)

Steps

  1. Finely chop the pepper, onion, garlic, and cilantro. Chop the tomatoes, carrot and potato into big chunks.
  2. Preheat a pot with oil and butter, add the pepper, onion, garlic, and cilantro and let them cook until golden.
  3. Add a cup of water, the tomatoes, and the tomato sauce into the pot. Turn the heat up and let the water boil, and the ingredients mix and soften, creating a broth.
  4. Once tomatoes are cooked, add more water, and the chickpeas, potatoes, and carrots. Add enough water to allow the added ingredients to cook.
  5. Season to taste with ground pepper, salt and ground garlic.
  6. Once everything is fully cooked, the soup will be ready to serve!
