Aztec Soup

Description

This recipe is for a natural Aztec Soup. This soup is made up from vegetables completely! The end product should be made up of a mixture of different vegetables (for seasoning), but mainly consist of tomatoes - because it's a tomato soup. Besides, the soup is usually served with toppings such as cheese, tortilla chips, hot souce, and avocado! It's a fast recipe to cook, and a delicious (usually) vegetarian meal!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (4 o 5)
  • One Celery
  • One Red Pepper
  • One Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Powdered Pepper
  • Salt
  • Natural tomato souce
  • Hot sauce (Optional)
  • Jalapeno (Optional)
  • Avocado
  • Cheese
  • Tortilla Chips

Steps

  1. Boil water, and put all the veggies in. It would be best if you chopped the ingredients a bit, not tiny, just enough so they're not boiled as the whole vegetable.
  2. Once Vegetables are done, put them all in the blender. Fill half the blender with water, and mix thoroughly. The mixture should end up with a red-ish look.
  3. Fill a cooking pot with the liquid mixture. Add a bit of tomato sauce, and season to your taste. Wait until the soup boils.
  4. Chop the cheese (skip this step if you bought shredded mozzarella).
  5. Chop the avocado into slices or small chunks.
  6. Finally, serve the soup, and add into it the cheese, avocado, tortilla chips, and any other sides you'd like!
